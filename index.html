Site about mountains!

Even a first-grader knows that the highest mountain in Ukraine is Hoverla, which has 2,061 m. This peak becomes a real mecca for tourists, not only for the first time in the Carpathians, but also for the first time as tourists: climbing Hoverla attracts even those who never I did not see with my own eyes a tourist backpack, as, in fact, the mountains. The first tourist route to the top of Hoverla was laid in 1880. There are four main routes to the mountain. Petros is the penultimate peak on the Montenegrin ridge. The mountain is spectacular and suitable for climbing at any time of year. Just note that climbing the winter Petros is not a task for beginners: such a hike is given a category of difficulty 2-A (not the most difficult route, but without the appropriate equipment, experience and a qualified instructor it is better not to stumble here).

Mount Spytsia

(1,863 m), Montenegrin ridge The name "Spitz" describes well the mood of this mountain. Natural stone mounds look like giant towers. Located at an altitude of more than 1800 meters, they impress the viewer with their scale, forming entire stone corridors. Each block is up to 40 meters high.

The daredevils will definitely demand a dose of adrenaline and climb the giant rocks, but be very careful, because the stone mounds are sharp as knives, and are at breakneck heights. It is better to find a good angle and take an accurate photo from a safe place. Do you still want to climb up? Come as part of an alpine group or with climbers - both are sometimes trained in Spitz. Here, however, it is much harder to carry equipment than on the popular for climbing Dovbush Rocks, so suddenly to find a group on the rocks - a great fortune.
